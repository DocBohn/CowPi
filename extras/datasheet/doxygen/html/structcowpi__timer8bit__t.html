Detailed Description

Structure for 8-bit timer/counter (TIMER0 or TIMER1).

The timer/counter interrupt mask register (TIMSKx) and the timer/counter interrupt flag register (TIFRx) are not part of this structure. Neither are the asynchronous status register (ASSR) nor the general timer/counter control register (GTCCR).
