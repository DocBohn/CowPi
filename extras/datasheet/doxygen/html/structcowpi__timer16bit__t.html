Detailed Description

Structure for 16-bit timer/counter (TIMER1).

The timer/counter interrupt mask register (TIMSKx) and the timer/counter interrupt flag register (TIFRx) are not part of this structure. Does not include the general timer/counter control register (GTCCR).
